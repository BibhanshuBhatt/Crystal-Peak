Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know for your exceptional stay

About Chopta

How can I make a reservation?
You can make a reservation through our elegant website, mobile app, or by calling our dedicated concierge team. We also provide personalized booking assistance for special occasions and exclusive group reservations.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 48 hours before arrival receive full refunds. Premium suites and exclusive packages require 72-hour notice. Our flexible policies ensure peace of mind for our valued guests.
Can I modify my booking details?
Absolutely. Modifications are welcomed subject to availability and any applicable rate adjustments. Our guest services team will gladly assist with all reservation changes.

Hotel Services

What premium amenities are included?
Every room features complimentary high-speed WiFi, 24/7 room service, Egyptian cotton linens, luxury spa amenities, and full access to our wellness center, infinity pool, and personal concierge services.
Do you provide airport transfers?
Yes, we offer premium chauffeur services with advance reservations. Suite guests enjoy complimentary luxury transfers, while all guests can access our exclusive transport service at competitive rates.
Is valet parking available?
Complimentary valet service is provided for all guests. We also offer secure self-parking facilities and dedicated electric vehicle charging stations for your convenience.

Nearby Places

What dining experiences do you offer?
We feature three Michelin-starred restaurants, an artisanal cocktail lounge, and 24-hour gourmet room service. Private dining experiences and exclusive chef's table arrangements are available by appointment.
Can you accommodate special dietary needs?
Our award-winning culinary team expertly accommodates all dietary preferences including vegan, gluten-free, kosher, and bespoke nutritional requirements with advance notice.
Do you host private celebrations?
We specialize in creating unforgettable events in our sophisticated venues. From intimate weddings to corporate galas, our events team provides comprehensive planning and world-class catering.

Policies & Information

What are your arrival and departure times?
Standard check-in begins at 3:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Early arrivals and extended departures are accommodated based on availability, with possible additional charges.
Do you welcome pets?
We warmly welcome well-behaved companions with advance arrangements. Our pet concierge service includes luxury bedding, gourmet treats, and professional walking services for your cherished pets.
What health and safety protocols are maintained?
We maintain exceptional safety standards with 24/7 security, contactless service options, rigorous sanitization protocols, and comprehensive health measures ensuring complete guest confidence and wellbeing.

Need Personal Assistance?

Our distinguished concierge team is available around the clock to provide personalized support for all your needs
