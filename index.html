About Crystal Peak Camp

About

Crystal Peak Camp is located close to Chopta Road, making it easy to reach by car or bike. Restaurants, shops, and even a hospital are nearby for your convenience. It’s the perfect spot for a comfortable and worry-free stay.

We provide everything guests need, from cozy tents to basic essentials, all at affordable prices. Our camp is well-maintained and in a safe area. You can enjoy your trip without having to worry about comfort or safety. Surrounded by nature, our camp offers stunning views of the mountains and fresh greenery.
